THE WANDERER



A Isometric/ 3rd Person roguelike, dungeon crawler game whereas the protagonist traverses to different levels of increasingly difficult rounds trying to get to the top of the whole dungeon fighting different enemies and finding various amounts of loot while trying to survive using only his wits and items that will only last in a set amount of levels.

This Project was developed with Level Design, AI Development and Randomly Generated Objects in mind. With that said, I lead the project with a group of 3 programmers. I had ample work done on AI and Level Design. I was also in-charge of UI and level generation. ​

NOT FOR THE FAINT OF HEART



Not for the Faint of Heart is a virtual reality horror game where there is only one thing the player has to keep in mind- Escape. This project was made in Unity3D. The assets were created in 3ds max for 3D model, and Adobe Illustrator for UI. I was in-charge of VR implementation and Level Design of the game. I was also the lead game designer and was in control of the different design aspects of the game. This game was built for HTC Vive and Oculus Rift.

PARTY KINGDOMS



4 Characters to fight against each other and to claim power by exploring dungeons. A multiplayer game where we fight free for all and find power-ups on dungeons. As a party game, fun and fast-paced action was kept in mind. I was in charge of creating the UI, Creating AI Behaviours and Mobile Controls.

CATCH THE SHEEP



Catch the Sheep is a mobile game built for android. It's an arcade-like game where the main focus is to collect different types of sheep. The art-style was akin to a cartoon that kids would watch everyday. The game is built on Unity and assets like sound and art are customely created by me.
